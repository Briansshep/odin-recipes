Mexican Stir Fry

Description

This Mexican stir fry recipe is quick and easy. You can customize with whatever your favorite ingredients are. For me a like to make it spicy so feel free to use less seasoning.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 yellow pepper
  • 1 red pepper
  • Taco seasoning
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Sour cream
  • Hot sauce
  • Tortilla chips
  1. Brown ground beef in pan at medium heat.
  2. While browning beef, chop up onion and peppers into strips.
  3. Combine ingredients and add at least 3 tbps of taco seasoning.
  4. Once cooked through, add lettuce, sour cream, hot sauce, and whatever other ingredients desired.
  5. Enjoy!
