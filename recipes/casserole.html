Casserole

Description

This breakfast casserole dish is one of the easiest dishes you can make. All you need are some eggs, your favorite cheese, crescent rolls, and ground sausage. Whisk your eggs in a bowl, cook your sausage, and combine everything together. It makes for a great meal to take to a party or just to start your weekend off right.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 2 packages of pillsbury crescent rolls
  • 1 cup of cheese (I prefer cheddar or colby jack)
  • 1 lb of ground sausage (I use maple sausage from Kroger or Walmart)
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Grease a pyrex pan. Unroll crescent rolls and lay them at the bottom of the pan.
  3. Brown sausage and spread it over the crescent rolls.
  4. Whisk eggs together. Pour over the sausage then cover in cheese.
  5. Pop in oven for 25-30 minutes. Let sit a few minutes and enjoy.
