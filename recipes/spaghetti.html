Simple Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti recipe is simple and tasty. It only takes about a half hour to make and is good for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Box of spaghetti noodles
  • Bag of meatballs
  • Spaghetti sauce
  1. Fill a pot with water and set to boil. Add spaghetti noodles once boiling.
  2. Cook meatballs on medium heat.
  3. Add spaghetti sauce to meatballs and let simmer.
  4. Drain water from noodles then mix noodles with sauce.
